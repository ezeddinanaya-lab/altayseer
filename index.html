Our Story

Craftsmanship Born from Passion

Founded in 1998 in the heart of the UAE, Al Tayseer Wooden Industries has grown from a small workshop into one of the region's most trusted names in fine wooden furniture and interior woodwork.

Our artisans combine age-old joinery techniques with modern precision machinery, using only responsibly sourced hardwoods — from rich Arabian Oak to imported Teak and Walnut — to produce pieces that endure generations.

How We Work

From Vision to Reality

01
Consultation & Design

We meet you in person or virtually to understand your space, style, and requirements. Our designers produce detailed 3D renders for approval.

02
Material Selection

Choose from our curated library of hardwoods, veneers, and finishes. We guide you to the perfect match for durability and aesthetics.

03
Precision Crafting

Our skilled craftsmen build each piece by hand, using CNC technology for precision joints and traditional hand-finishing for warmth and character.

04
Delivery & Installation

We deliver and install everything ourselves — no third parties — ensuring your furniture is placed and assembled exactly to specification.
